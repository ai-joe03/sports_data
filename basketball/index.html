How xEFG% is calculated
Each shot is assigned an expected eFG% from its physical distance and angle to the basket.
  • Restricted area ≈ 62%
  • Paint 50% → 40% (linear with distance)
  • Corner 3 ≈ 58%
  • Above-break 3 ≈ 52%, with an additional penalty for each extra metre beyond 7 m
Shots are coloured relative to the season league average.
